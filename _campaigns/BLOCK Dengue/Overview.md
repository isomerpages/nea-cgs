<h3>A BITE FROM AN&nbsp;<em>AEDES</em>&nbsp;MOSQUITO MAY BE LITTLE BUT LETHAL.</h3>
<p>Dengue can potentially lead to serious health consequences, such as:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Bleeding from the nose or gums</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Persistent vomiting</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sudden onset of fever for 2 to 7 days</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Joint and muscle pain</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Skin rashes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Easy bruising of the skin</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Unlike Covid-19, there is no effective vaccine against dengue.&nbsp;So
everyone needs to play their part to #BLOCKDengue by depriving mosquitoes
of potential breeding habitats.</p>
