<h3>Join us at the <strong>Waste Less Supermart</strong> at 313@Somerset on 26 and 27 April 2025!</h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

<h4>What can you expect?</h4>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Discover Waste Less Tips</strong> –
Learn simple, actionable ways to reduce disposables and food wastage in
everyday situations, from grocery shopping to dining out.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Understand the Impact</strong> –
See how small lifestyle changes can not only present cost savings, but
also contribute to a larger environmental impact, helping to build a cleaner,
greener Singapore.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Explore SYTWL Partners’ Initiatives</strong> –
Get to know the various sustainability efforts led by SYTWL partners, including
innovative waste reduction programs, eco-friendly alternatives, and reward
systems that encourage sustainable habits. <em>(Psst. Look out for special SYTWL promo codes to reward you for wasting less!)</em>
</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Complimentary drinks when you BYO</strong> –
Bring your own reusable bottle or cup and redeem a Starbucks iced coffee
or tea drink!</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Redeem</strong> a SYTWL collapsible
silicone cup.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Catch the live filming of our reality
series, <strong>Waste Less? Say YES!</strong>, featuring Avery, Chen Ning,
Germaine and Glenn Yong!</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Participate in free workshops </strong>–
Design and take home your very own handy carabiner made of bottle caps.</p>
