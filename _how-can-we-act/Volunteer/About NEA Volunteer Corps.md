<h4>About NEA Volunteer Corps</h4>
<p>Protecting the environment and upkeeping good public health and hygiene
is the responsibility of everyone in the community. As individuals, we
can all do this by first personally adopting environmentally friendly lifestyle
habits, as well as good personal hygiene practices.</p>
<p>As part of the NEA Volunteer Corps, you can go a step further, by helping
everyone around us become much more aware of this shared responsibility.
You can play a big part in guiding others to do their part, so that collectively,
we can all continue to enjoy a Clean &amp; Green Singapore.</p>
<p>Volunteer opportunities offered by the NEA Volunteer Corps include public
outreach deployments to promote dengue prevention, a litter-free Singapore,
clean public toilets, reducing waste and recycling, conducting patrols
to engage environmental offenders, and auditing your own residential estate’s
cleanliness.</p>
<p>Join the NEA Volunteer Corps and make a difference today! Read on to find
out more!</p>
<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>To lead by example and inspire others to practice good public health and
hygiene, as well as lead an environmentally friendly lifestyle</p>
<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>A clean, green and sustainable environment for Singapore, supported by
a community of active volunteers</p>
